RELEVENT PROBLEMS AND ALGORITMS

PROBLEMS:

  1. How can we identify the most popular or trending products that would attract customers?
  2. How can we find the shortest distance from the source to the destination for product delivery to enhance customer reliability?

ALGORITHMS TO SOLVE THE PROBLEM:

  1. The main focus of marketing is understanding customer needs, which can be achieved by identifying the most popular products and advertising them effectively. This can be done by sorting products based on the highest sales, allowing you to prioritize the most in-demand items.

    One of the efficient algorithm is MERGE SORT.

  2. The market is highly competitive, and to sustain success, one must be the best service provider. To minimize customer wait times, the delivery of items must be fast. This can be achieved by identifying the shortest distance between the source and destination, ensuring quicker and more efficient deliveries.

    One of the efficient algorithm is Dijkstra's Algorithm.

RELEVENT PROBLEMS AND ALGORITMS TO SOLVE IT

PROBLEMS:

In our designed city, "SWARGANSH".
  1. We have allocated a total of 150 sq. km for agriculture. This vast area is intended for cultivating a variety of agricultural products. How can we effectively distribute these products across different categories for optimal utilization?
  2. We have several APMCs located across the city to facilitate the transfer of agricultural products. How can we determine the shortest path between these locations to save time and resources?

ALGORITHMS TO SOLVE THE PROBLEM:

  1. The agricultural products can be categorized and separated based on their cost or type. This can be achieved by assigning a unique value to each agricultural product, sorting them accordingly, and then separating the unique items into distinct categories.

    One of the efficient sorting technique is QUICK SORT.

  2. To determine the shortest path between APMCs and agricultural areas (farms).

    One of the efficient algorithm is Dijkstra's Algorithm.
