Business Cases for Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs)

Business Case 1: Renewable Energy Solutions

SDG Target: 7.2 - Increase the share of renewable energy in the global energy mix by 2030.

SDG Indicators:

  • Indicator 7.2.1: Renewable energy share in the total energy consumption.
  • Indicator 7.2.2: Growth rate of renewable energy consumption.

Business Goal: To increase the use of solar and wind energy sources, reduce reliance on fossil fuels, and contribute to cleaner energy production.

Targets:

  • Install solar panels on 10,000 residential and commercial buildings by 2025.
  • Reduce carbon emissions by 20% by 2027 by increasing the renewable energy adoption rate.
  • Provide renewable energy solutions to underserved communities in rural areas by 2030.

Key Performance Indicators (KPIs):

  • Percentage increase in renewable energy adoption year-on-year.
  • Reduction in carbon footprint measured in tons of CO2 avoided.
  • Number of new partnerships with local governments and businesses to expand renewable energy infrastructure.

Business Case 2: Sustainable Agriculture and Food Security

SDG Target: 2.4 - Ensure sustainable food production systems and implement resilient agricultural practices by 2030.

SDG Indicators:

  • Indicator 2.4.1: Proportion of agricultural area under productive and sustainable practices.
  • Indicator 2.4.2: Average income of small-scale food producers, by sex and indigenous status.

Business Goal: To promote organic farming, reduce the use of harmful pesticides, and enhance food security for local communities.

Targets:

  • Convert 50% of the farming land to organic practices by 2028.
  • Increase food security in 5 rural communities by providing sustainable farming equipment and training by 2027.
  • Collaborate with 100 local farmers to adopt climate-resilient agricultural techniques by 2030.

Key Performance Indicators (KPIs):

  • Percentage of agricultural land transitioned to sustainable farming practices.
  • Income increase of small-scale farmers participating in the program.
  • Reduction in food waste and improvement in local food distribution efficiency.

Business Case 3: Inclusive Education and Skill Development

SDG Target: 4.4 - Increase the number of youth and adults who have relevant skills for employment, decent jobs, and entrepreneurship by 2030.

SDG Indicators:

  • Indicator 4.4.1: Proportion of youth and adults with ICT skills.
  • Indicator 4.4.2: Proportion of youth and adults with skills for employment or entrepreneurship.

Business Goal: To provide accessible and inclusive educational opportunities to marginalized communities, focusing on digital skills and vocational training.

Targets:

  • Provide vocational training to 10,000 youth in underdeveloped areas by 2025.
  • Offer free online courses in digital literacy to 50,000 adults by 2027.
  • Collaborate with tech companies to offer internships and apprenticeships to 1,000 young people by 2028.

Key Performance Indicators (KPIs):

  • Number of students successfully completing training programs and securing jobs or starting businesses.
  • Increase in digital literacy rates among adults and youth in targeted regions.
  • Number of partnerships with companies to provide internships and employment opportunities.
